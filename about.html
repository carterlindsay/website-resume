About Me

Organized. Intentional. Driven.

Hi, I'm Lindsay Carter — a sophomore Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. I am very responsible and intentional with everything I do, and I am passionate about my education and serving in the community.

I am highly organized and self-motivated, and I genuinely enjoy growing and progressing on my own. My experiences — from leading sister missionaries in the Dominican Republic to assisting patients at a dental office and managing the front desk at a rheumatology clinic — have shaped me into someone who takes initiative and works hard in every role I take on.

I am a representative of Jesus Christ and love sharing the joy of His gospel with all. Whether in professional, academic, or community settings, I strive to bring my best self and make a positive difference wherever I am.
